    My strategy involves a combination of structured learning (courses, documentation), hands-on experience through personal projects and collaboration, staying updated with industry trends via blogs and communities, and seeking feedback and mentorship whenever possible.

      • Finish all Projects for this term timely.
      • Finalize thesis matters.
      • Seek first collaborative project/internship experiences.
    2. 1-2 Years

      Building & Integrating

      • Learn new languages related to chosen Thesis.
      • Continue Graphic Design career.
      • Further improve knowledge on database concepts (SQL/NoSQL).
      • Contribute meaningfully to team projects (Git flow).
      • Practice user research & user flow creation.
    3. 3-5 Years

      Proficiency & Specialization

      • Achieve full-stack development competency.
      • Explore potential specializations (e.g., performance, UX strategy, API design).
      • Start a professional career in graphic design.
      • Work on larger-scale, impactful projects.
    4. 5+ Years

      Leadership & Contribution

      • Consider technical leadership or senior design roles.
      • Contribute back to the community (mentoring, writing, open source).
      • Continuously adapt to evolving technologies.
      • Maintain a strong portfolio of diverse projects.
